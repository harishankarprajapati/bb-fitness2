About Us

Overall strength and movement

Program is designed to improve your overall functional fitness, making daily tasks and activities easier to perform. Our expert coaches will guide you through each session, ensuring proper technique and progression to help you achieve your goals.

  • 3 sessions per week, 60 minutes each
  • Small Training Groups: 4-6 persons
  • Combination of strength, stability, and power training exercises
  • Focus on multi-joint, full-body movements
  • Power Development and Metabolic Conditioning
